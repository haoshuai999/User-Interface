Who is Alexander the Great?
He was a king of a small Greek country called Kingdom of Macedon
Why he is "great"?
By the age of thirty he had created one of the largest empires of the ancient world, stretching from Greece to northwestern India
What's the impact of the expedition?
His expedition was bloody and cruel but it also promoted cultural diffusion and syncretism between the west and middle east in third century BC
